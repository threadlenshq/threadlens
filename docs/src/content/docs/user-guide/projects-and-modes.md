---
title: Projects and Modes
description: Understand ThreadLens projects and the current research and marketing mode distinction.
---


A project is the main workspace for a market, audience, niche, or product idea. Projects own queries, prompts, posts, reports, scout runs, and schedules.

## Research mode

Research mode focuses on finding pain points, repeated complaints, workarounds, and product opportunities. It is the primary self-hosted adoption path.

## Marketing mode

Marketing mode is for engagement and outreach workflows. Use it only when your goal is to act on already-understood opportunities rather than discover them.

## Project hygiene

- Keep one project focused on one audience or market.
- Disable noisy queries instead of deleting them immediately.
- Use reports to preserve conclusions from useful runs.
